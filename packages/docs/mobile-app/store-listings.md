# 🏪 Store Listings

A great store listing convinces customers to download your app. This page provides templates and guidance for your App Store and Google Play listings.

## 📝 App Description Template

Customize this template with your restaurant's details:

### 📋 Short Description (Google Play — 80 characters max)

```
Order food for delivery or pickup from [Restaurant Name]. Fast & easy!
```

### 📄 Full Description

```
[Restaurant Name] — your favorite meals, just a tap away.

Browse our full menu, customize your order, and get it delivered to your door
or ready for pickup. No more waiting on hold or repeating your order over the
phone.

🍔 BROWSE & ORDER
• Browse our complete menu with photos and descriptions
• Filter by category — appetizers, mains, desserts, drinks, and more
• Customize items with your preferred options and toppings
• Special dietary needs? Check allergen information on every item

🚚 DELIVERY & PICKUP
• Order for delivery right to your address
• Or choose pickup and skip the wait
• Schedule orders in advance for the perfect timing
• Track your order status in real-time

💳 EASY CHECKOUT
• Pay securely with credit card, PayPal, or cash
• Apply coupon codes for discounts
• Earn loyalty points with every order
• Guest checkout — no account required to place your first order

📅 TABLE RESERVATIONS
• Book a table for any occasion
• Choose your preferred date, time, and party size
• View available time slots instantly
• Manage and view your upcoming reservations

👤 YOUR ACCOUNT
• View your complete order history
• Reorder your favorites quickly
• Manage your profile and delivery addresses

🔔 STAY UPDATED
• Get notified when your order status changes
• Know the moment your food is ready for pickup
• Never miss a special offer

🌍 Available in English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, and Portuguese.

Questions or feedback? Contact us at [your email] or visit [your website].
```

### 🍎 Apple App Store Subtitle (30 characters max)

```
Order Food & Reserve Tables
```

### 🔍 Keywords (Apple — 100 characters max, comma-separated)

```
food,delivery,order,restaurant,pickup,pizza,reservation,menu,takeout,dining
```

Replace `pizza` with your cuisine type.

## 📸 Screenshot Strategy

Screenshots are the most important part of your listing — they're the first thing customers see.

### 🖼️ Recommended Screenshots (5-8 screens)

| # | Screen | What to Show |
|---|--------|-------------|
| 1️⃣ | Home screen | Your restaurant branding and welcome message |
| 2️⃣ | Menu | Categories and menu items with appetizing photos |
| 3️⃣ | Item detail | A popular item with customization options |
| 4️⃣ | Cart | Items in cart with subtotal |
| 5️⃣ | Order tracking | Real-time status tracker |
| 6️⃣ | Reservations | Table booking screen |
| 7️⃣ | Order history | Past orders list |
| 8️⃣ | Account | Profile with loyalty points |

### 💡 Screenshot Tips

- 📷 **Show real data**: Use your actual menu items and photos, not placeholder content
- 📝 **Add text overlays**: Brief captions above each screenshot explaining the feature (e.g., "Browse Our Full Menu", "Track Your Order in Real-Time")
- 🎨 **Use your brand colors**: Match your restaurant's color scheme
- ⭐ **First screenshot is crucial**: This is the one shown in search results — make it your best

## 🖼️ Feature Graphic (Google Play)

Google Play requires a **1024 x 500 px** feature graphic. This banner appears at the top of your listing.

Design tips:
- 🏷️ Use your restaurant's logo prominently
- 📝 Include a tagline or call-to-action
- 🎨 Use your brand colors
- 📱 Keep it simple — it appears small on phones

## 📱 App Icon

Both stores display your app icon prominently. Requirements:

| Store | Size | Format | Notes |
|-------|------|--------|-------|
| 🤖 Google Play | 512 x 512 px | PNG | No transparency |
| 🍎 Apple App Store | 1024 x 1024 px | PNG | No transparency, no rounded corners (Apple adds them) |

The icon should be:
- ✅ Simple and recognizable at small sizes
- ✅ Distinctive from competitor apps
- 🎨 Using your brand colors
- ❌ Not containing text (it's too small to read)

## 🔒 Privacy Policy

Both stores require a privacy policy URL. Here's a template you can adapt:

```
Privacy Policy for [Restaurant Name] App

Last updated: [Date]

[Restaurant Name] ("we", "us", "our") operates the [Restaurant Name]
mobile application.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When you use our app, we may collect:
- Account information: name, email address, phone number
- Order information: items ordered, delivery address, order history
- Payment information: processed securely by Stripe/PayPal (we do not
  store your card details)
- Device information: device type, push notification tokens

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information to:
- Process and deliver your orders
- Send order status notifications
- Manage your account and loyalty points
- Improve our services

DATA SHARING

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:
- Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal) to process transactions
- Delivery partners (if applicable) to deliver your orders

DATA RETENTION

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request
deletion by contacting us.

YOUR RIGHTS

You can:
- Access your personal data through the app
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Opt out of marketing communications

CONTACT US

For privacy questions, contact us at:
Email: [your email]
Address: [your business address]
```

Host this on your website (e.g., `https://yourdomain.com/privacy`) and link to it in both store listings.

## 💬 Responding to Reviews

After publishing, customers will leave ratings and reviews. Responding shows you care:

- ⭐ **Positive reviews**: Thank the customer briefly. "Thanks for the kind words! We're glad you enjoy ordering through the app."
- 🐛 **Negative reviews about the app**: Acknowledge the issue and provide a fix timeline. "Sorry about that! We've fixed the checkout issue in our latest update."
- 🍽️ **Negative reviews about the food**: Be professional and move the conversation offline. "We're sorry your meal wasn't up to our standards. Please email us at [email] and we'll make it right."
