# 🏢 Developer Accounts

Before you can publish your app, you need developer accounts with Apple and Google. This process involves identity verification and can take a few days, so start early.

## 🤖 Google Play Developer Account

### 📋 What You Need

- 📧 A Google account (Gmail)
- 💳 A credit/debit card for the one-time $25 registration fee
- 📱 A valid phone number for verification
- 🪪 Government-issued ID (for identity verification)

### 📝 Step-by-Step Registration

1. 🌐 **Go to the Google Play Console**: Visit [play.google.com/console/signup](https://play.google.com/console/signup)

2. 🔑 **Sign in** with the Google account you want to use as the developer account. This should ideally be your restaurant's business Google account, not a personal one.

3. 🏢 **Choose account type**:
   - Select **Organization** if you have a registered business
   - Select **Personal** if you don't have a formal business entity

   ::: tip 💡
   An Organization account shows your business name on the Play Store, which looks more professional. A Personal account shows your personal name.
   :::

4. 📝 **Enter developer details**:
   - **Developer name**: Your restaurant name (e.g., "Shutter" or "My Restaurant LLC")
   - **Contact email**: A professional email address
   - **Phone number**: Your business or personal phone
   - **Website**: Your restaurant's website (e.g., `https://order.yourdomain.com`)

5. 💳 **Pay the registration fee**: $25 USD one-time payment via credit card

6. 🪪 **Complete identity verification**: Google requires ID verification for new accounts. Upload:
   - A government-issued photo ID (passport, driver's license)
   - You may also need to provide a selfie

7. ⏳ **Wait for approval**: Verification typically takes **2-7 business days**. You'll receive an email when approved.

### ✅ After Approval

Once approved, you'll have access to the Google Play Console at [play.google.com/console](https://play.google.com/console). Keep this open — you'll return here to create your app listing and upload builds.

## 🍎 Apple Developer Program

### 📋 What You Need

- 🔑 An Apple ID (create one at [appleid.apple.com](https://appleid.apple.com) if you don't have one)
- 💳 A credit/debit card for the $99/year membership
- 🖥️ A Mac, iPhone, or iPad to complete enrollment
- 🏢 For organizations: a D-U-N-S Number (free to obtain)

### 👤 Individual vs. Organization

| | Individual | Organization |
|---|---|---|
| **💰 Cost** | $99/year | $99/year |
| **🏪 Shows on App Store** | Your personal name | Your organization name |
| **📋 Requires** | Apple ID, ID verification | Apple ID, D-U-N-S Number, legal entity |
| **⏳ Approval time** | 1-2 days | 1-4 weeks |
| **🎯 Best for** | Solo restaurant owners | Registered businesses |

::: tip 💡 Recommendation
If you have a registered business (LLC, Corp, etc.), enroll as an **Organization**. It shows your business name on the App Store and looks more professional. If you're a sole proprietor without a registered entity, **Individual** is fine.
:::

### 🔢 Get a D-U-N-S Number (Organizations Only)

A D-U-N-S Number is a unique identifier for your business, issued by Dun & Bradstreet. Apple requires this for Organization accounts.

1. 🔍 **Check if you already have one**: Visit [developer.apple.com/enroll/duns-lookup](https://developer.apple.com/enroll/duns-lookup/) and search for your business.

2. 📝 **If you don't have one**: Request a free D-U-N-S Number through the same page. Provide:
   - Legal business name
   - Business address
   - Phone number
   - Contact person information

3. ⏳ **Wait for issuance**: This typically takes **3-7 business days**, but can take up to 30 days in some regions. You'll receive the number via email.

### 📝 Step-by-Step Enrollment

1. 🌐 **Go to Apple Developer**: Visit [developer.apple.com/programs/enroll](https://developer.apple.com/programs/enroll/)

2. 🔑 **Sign in** with your Apple ID

3. ▶️ **Start enrollment**: Click "Start Your Enrollment"

4. 🏢 **Choose entity type**:
   - **Individual / Sole Proprietor** — uses your personal name
   - **Organization** — uses your business name (requires D-U-N-S Number)

5. 📝 **Fill in your information**:
   - For **Individuals**: legal name, address, phone number
   - For **Organizations**: legal entity name, D-U-N-S Number, business address, website, signing authority contact

6. 📄 **Review and agree** to the Apple Developer Program License Agreement

7. 💳 **Pay the membership fee**: $99 USD per year, paid via Apple ID payment method

8. ⏳ **Wait for approval**:
   - **Individuals**: Usually approved within **48 hours**
   - **Organizations**: Apple verifies your D-U-N-S Number and business identity. This can take **1-4 weeks**. Apple may call the phone number associated with your D-U-N-S Number to verify.

### ✅ After Approval

You'll have access to:
- 🏪 [App Store Connect](https://appstoreconnect.apple.com) — where you create app listings and upload builds
- 🔧 [Apple Developer Portal](https://developer.apple.com/account/) — where you manage certificates and provisioning profiles

## 📦 Expo Account

Expo is the build system used by the Shutter mobile app. You need a free account.

1. 🌐 Visit [expo.dev/signup](https://expo.dev/signup)
2. 📝 Sign up with GitHub, Google, or email
3. 🆓 No payment required — the free tier includes 30 builds per month
